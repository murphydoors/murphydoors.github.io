About Us

As the name implies, we are a full service door automation company. Complete Automatic Doors of Colorado services the Pueblo and Colorado Springs areas, but we will travel all across Colorado to make sure our customers are taken care of. We offer sales, service, and installation of a multitude of commercial door products. We are market driven and ready to meet the growing needs of the commercial door and security market in this area. With experience in the Kansas City, New Mexico, and Colorado markets, never have we encountered a problem with a door that did not have a solution. We look forward to work with you and your company.
